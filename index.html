All paths lead back to me.

“What if every living thing in the universe — past, present, and future — is you?”
— Nitshanchai

What is EERT?

EERT is a soul philosophy.

It teaches that every person, every living thing, is part of the same soul — scattered across time and space, learning through different lives, slowly remembering who we really are.

We’re not separate. We’re not strangers. We’re all pieces of the same whole, walking different paths — but heading toward the same place: Home.

EERT is not a religion. It doesn’t ask you to believe in anything. It asks you to remember.

  • Every act of kindness is an act of healing yourself.
  • Every harm you cause, you will one day feel — because one day, you’ll become the one who was hurt.
  • Every lesson, every challenge, every person you meet… is part of your soul’s return to wholeness.

To live by EERT is to live with awareness.

  • What am I learning in this moment?
  • How can I treat others as if they are me?
  • What would life look like if we all remembered?

EERT is about becoming whole again. Not by being perfect — but by being honest. By choosing compassion. By seeing every step, even the painful ones, as part of the way back.

You don’t need to have all the answers. You just need to remember the truth that’s already inside you.

You are not broken. You are returning.
