EERT is for you — because it always has been.

About EERT

A philosophy born from silence, suffering, and soul awakening.

It was created by Nitshanchai to answer the quiet ache inside us:
“Why do I feel like I’ve lived this before?”

My Story

Why I Remember – The Story of Nitshanchai

I didn’t grow up with bedtime stories — I grew up with shouting.
My earliest memory is of trying to stop two adults from tearing each other apart.

Through silence, streetlight survival, martial discipline, and eventual grief —
I found remembrance.

EERT is my offering, so others never have to forget themselves as deeply as I did.
